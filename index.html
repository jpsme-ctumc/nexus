Welcome to Project Nexus

Project OJT NEXUS aims to help Third-Year BSME students transition to their On-the-Job Training by providing a centralized directory of verified company details, application modes, and firsthand experiences gathered from Alumni and 4th-Year students.

This initiative addresses challenges like blind hunting for companies, lack of professional networks, and uncertainty about requirements. Our goal is to ensure equal access and a smoother OJT journey for all aspiring mechanical engineers.

Disclaimer

The information provided in Project OJT NEXUS is intended for reference and guidance purposes only. All company details, application processes, and shared experiences are compiled from alumni and student contributions; therefore, users are expected to utilize the data responsibly and professionally.

Inclusion of a company in the directory does not guarantee the availability of OJT slots or acceptance of trainees. Students must directly contact and inquire with the respective companies to confirm current internship opportunities, requirements, and application procedures.
